User Guide

"Modules""

Our "modules" whether it is game, "Stories", viewing video (on YouTube) or "Gems" etc. some do not require connection to the internet. Your connection to the internet is off, but you still get to play in some modules.

Modules that require no connection to the internet are:

  • Gems
  • Listen: tap "My Downloads"
  • Word Puzzles
  • Image Puzzles
  • Lesson of a Day
  • Color Meditation
  • My Hindu Calendar (assuming your location was downloaded)
  • Journal
  • Settings

All the rest of the modules require connection to the internet.

Your Bandwidth

Your bandwidth, that is a connection to the internet, be it fast WiFi, a slow WiFi or cellular connection, whether is it a high bandwidth or a cellular connection that is very little. Take for example, your in a car, going through a tunnel - cellular connection is very bad. Then, you are going to a place where cellular connection is high. The modules of that require connection to the internet, maybe fast, or will take some patient or they won't work at all.

"Journal" and Your Favorites

  • Overtime, you will have many things entered to your "Journal". Tap the star on the left of an entry to make it a "Favorite".
  • Tap on "Filter by Favorites" in the "Journal" to show only "Favorites".
  • Now, "Favorites" will also appear as "My Menu" in the upper left navigator of the home screen. Use the three bar. Go directly to audio saved to the "Journal" .

Audio

  • Loading Audio: Go to "Listen". There will be "Collections" - various talks, music, songs, chants or audio books. Tap one these. A list of titles will appear. Tap title and then audio will play. A title will show up. Follow by lyrics or a transcripts if it exists. Otherwise a picture of Siva will appear. Go to "Help" on the "Listen" for more details about saving to your "Journal".

  • Load Audio to Favorites in the "Journal" : You can connect directly to the audio by tapping "Favorites" after you have made a " "Journal" " entry. That way you can hear a talk directly from the Home screen. You go to:

  • Periodically, to gain space on your phone, you can delete the audio files you don't need. Tap the word "Delete" icon in lower right corner. You will be taken to a screen which shows you the file name of something you can delete. Tap the lower if left arrow to go back to "My Downloads".

  • It is recommended that you are in a Wi-Fi area to download talks or audiobooks that will take lots of time. Or, if have a patience, you can do something that will to 15 minutes to download. Tap on "Loading Collectiob" the turn it off.

  • "Speaker" Icon: The audio is set to play while you go to other activities and other app. In the app, on other screens, a small "Speaker" icon appears. If the audio is not playing, tap it once to play. If audio is playing, tap in once to end it.

  • We have lots of audio feature requests and are working to implement them over time. Please go to Feedback under settings and send us your suggestions. This best method to get back audio that you like: download to My Audio, then record your choices in the "Journal" .

Stories

We have completely re-made the slides option. They are going to be landscape or portrait. But, if you are on the images, if you tapped on them, they are enlarged. Now, you can look around by dragging the photo with your finger. But if they are enlarged, they can't be switched to landscape or portrait. But, if you tap/click 4 times, they land in their original (small) size, and they will go to landscape or portrait.

Exploring Images

In almost all contexts try:

  • Tap once: The image will shrink to show all.

  • Tap again: The image is full size and centered.

  • Hold down and drag: Scroll around to see the rest of the image.

  • Tap on the Nataraja icon on the "portal" screen, to see a different image of Lord Siva
