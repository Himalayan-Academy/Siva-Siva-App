Why is the Path to Siva so important?

There are 68 Lessons in the Path to Siva. They bring you close to the living sampradaya at Kauai Aadheenam. We, as 21st century people from all over the world, could well be called the heirs of the "Adi Saivas," "First to worship Siva," a tradition that goes beyond time and history.

This book is a short summary of the beliefs, practices and theology of Saiva Siddhanta, one of the great streams of the Hindu religion. It captures the life-long mission and teachings of Satguru Sivaya Subramuniyaswami [1927-2001]. To bring the purest Saivism to the West, he founded Kauai Aadheenam on Kauai, Hawaii, where he carried on the teachings of his Satguru, Siva Yogaswami [1872-1964] from Sri Lanka.

Reading

The first of two ways to read this book is from front to back like turning the page. By clicking the Previous Arrow, top left and Forward Arrow, top right, you can move through the lessons one by one.

There is also a Table of Contents which you access by tapping the text on any page to reveal a navigation bar. At the top right, there is an icon with the three bars. Tap this and the Table of Contents appears which lets you quickly move to any lesson. There is no such navigation for the Glossary, About and Photo Index.

Photo Index

Another tool for navigation is a photo index, accessed by clicking on the left arrow in the navigation bar. You can scroll through the photos and lesson names to get to the one you want to study. Tap on any photo to read the lesson.

Reading Captions

Every lesson has an image with a hidden caption. Tap the image bar to reveal the caption. Tap again to hide the caption.

Journal

Click on Journal. It will take you to your current lesson, let's call it "Lesson 19." It will record that lesson. After that, it will take you to a decision: you can exit the Path to Siva or you Cancel to stay with Path to Siva. If you go for a couple days without using the SivaSiva App, once you go back, click Journal, it will take you immediately back to "Lesson 19".

Glossary

When the Table of Contents is open, you can click on the toggle switch to "Show Defined Words." It will turn green and return to the text. All the words in the Glossary will now be highlighted in yellow. Long-press a word to see its definition. Tap again to close the definition window. You can turn off the highlighting the same way.

Going Away

Click on Journal or Photo Index, click on Left Arrow. It will give you the choice, to exit the Path to Siva or Cancel. Click the Table of Contents. Line two will show you "Siva Portal, Go Home." If you click that, Siva Portal will appear.
