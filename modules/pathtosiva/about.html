Reading

There are 68 Lessons in the Path to Siva. There are two ways to read this book: from front to back like any other book, or by reading lessons related to topics that interest you. Previous Arrow, top left and Forward Arrow, top right.

At the top second from the right, an icon with the three bars. Tap this and a Table of Contents appears. The Table of Contents lets you quickly move from one page to another. There is no Table of Contents, for the Glossary, About and Photo Index.

Photo Index

The Photo Index lists all lessons in order. Tap on any photo to read the lesson.

Reading Captions

Every lesson has an image with a caption. Tap the colored bar under it to read the caption. Tap again to hide the caption.

Journal

Click on Journal. It will take your Lesson, lets call it "Lesson 19". If you go by it for several days, click Journal, it takes you to "Lesson 19". After that it will take you to a decision: you can exit the Path to Siva or you Cancel to stay with Path to Siva.

Glossary

Use the Table of Contents, "Show Defined Words". Click on the Round Ball at the top of Table of Contents. It will show a green color. Press on the Lesson, Table of Contents will go away. Then the words in the glossary will show with a hilite of yellow. Long-press a word with a hilite, see its definition. Then you can go to Table of Contents, at the top, click the Round Ball, it will show the hilite white. And the glossary will go away.

Going Away

Click on Journal or Photo Index, click on Left Arrow. It will give you the choice, to exit the Path to Siva or Cancel. Click the Table of Contents. Line two will show you "Siva Portal, Go Home". If you click that, Siva Portal will come up.
