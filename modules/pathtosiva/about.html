Reading

There are 68 Lessons in the Path to Siva. There are two ways to read this book: from front to back like any other book, or by reading lessons related to topics that interest you. Previous Arrow, top left and Forward Arrow, top right.

At the top second from the right, an icon with the three bars. Tap this and a Table of Contents appears. The Table of Contents lets you quickly move from one page to another. There is no Table of Contents, for the Glossary, About and Photo Index.

Photo Index

The Photo Index lists all about.htmllessons in order. Tap on any photo to read the lesson.

Reading Captions

Every lesson has an image with a caption. Tap the image or the colored bar under it to read the caption. Tap again to hide the caption.

p>Glossary

Tapping a word in the text will draw a red dotted line under it if the word is in the glossary. Long-press the word to see its definition.

Use the Table of Contents to move to other sections of the book, such as the Glossary or this About page. The Home menu item takes you back to the main Read portal in the app.

Glossary

Glossary definitions sometimes contain other terms also in the glossary. You can tap a word in a definition to see that word's definition.
