250 Publications

You will have 250 publications that you can read. Tap on a language, like "Gujarati". You will get a publication, "Gujarati, 12 Beliefs of Saivism". Tap that book. It will open with metadata. There is not much. Tap the link "Open Book" and it will take you to a browser/publication.

Tap on the left arrow. Then you get to the index. Go to the language. "English". Tap on "Dancing with Siva". You will get a lot of the metadata. Tap "Open Book" and it will take you the book, "Dancing with Siva".

You have ended with a page, say in "Chapter 1". If you don't have time today, but you would start at "Chapter 1" tomorrow? Tap on the Journal, second from left, while you are in "Chapter 1". If you go out, (tap on the left arrow) and look at the "Journal". There is an entry to go back to "Chapter 1". Tomorrow, go to the Journal. Tap on "Chapter 1". You will be taken to the publication in a browser. Keep reading. Then, you will have Journal entry that says "Chapter 2" which you can go through tomorrow.

In fact, you can create Journal entries for more publications. Delete any Journal entry which old.
