First, we need turn off the "Portrait Orientation Lock: Off". Because it prevents going "landscape".

Coloring

"DOWNLOAD" from the "Coloring". It will come up with black and white images. You will find this colorful icon at the bottom, second icon from the left. Click it.

You come to six icons along the bottom. They will be on a left edge if it is landscape.

Six icons:
Bottom or left edges

     -- Left Chevron: It will give you a dialog box "Do you want to save the image? YES | NO" and it will take you to the top of image.
     -- Eraser: you click to get rid of your color.
     -- Brush: if click once it will reveal a patterns. Click that once you decide. Then free frame.
     -- Bucket: the color goes into a line border
     -- Color Palette: That's the first thing you do -- it bring you a color palette. Choose a color that you like. It goes away. But the color is shown on the icon.
     -- Heart: you click that if you want to save that color. And go to work in the same image

Pick your Brush or Bucket: go to work!

Download from the Cloud

When you download from the cloud, they will tell you what size it is. If you live in a city or place with Wi-Fi everywhere, you be should to go ahead and download. But you may be looking at ten minutes for a download, turn it off if it gets too long. OR be patient, ten minutes may be good. If your phone is filling up, you have the chance for deleting them. Perhaps in a few weeks, when you gave grasped what the story is telling you.

Visual Slides / Text

They are made up of a visual image, followed by article which can be viewed as text only, followed by a image. Some images may have captions and some do not. There cases of images which may not have articles. We show the next image.

You will be first shown the image at its length or width of your phone. Take, for example, a panormic image. It will view as a tiny image. This a clue for tapping on an image. You will taken to a close up. Slide it around to take in, sometimes gorgeous or very informative, views an a image. Tapped it again leads you back to the screen where your next article or image is available.
