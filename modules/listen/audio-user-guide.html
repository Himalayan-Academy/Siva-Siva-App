User Guide

1. Download: you can Download the songs/inspired talk etc. the upper right icon. DO TRY the "My Download". That will take you a screen with download songs/inspired talk etc.

2. My Downloads: once you download the songs/inspired talk etc. it will be there in "My Download". While you the "listen" and you can take the Forward and Back, by five seconds or you can go the under controller and go forward/Back

3. Shortcuts: and, save a copy to Shortcuts. That way you can play it tomorrow!

"Collections"

Depending on your bandwidth, if you click on first two "Collections" - "Recent Additions" and "Bodhinathas Lastest" - we go to the distance server. When it comes up "Loading Collection" it may fast, if you have WiFi. Or take time with cellular connection. Be patient! It will give you a list of titles. Or tap on "Load "Collections"" to tell it to stop.

The other "Collections", for example, "Classical Artist", "Popular Tunes", "Ganesha Bhajans" will go to local database. They will not to take time to load a list of titles. But they will take time to load the file when you go to play it. For the songs, which are short, they will be fast. For the songs/audio books/inspired talks which take 10 minutes or longer to play. They too will say "Loading Collection". Be patient or tap "Loading "Collections"" to get to stop.

With cellular connection, if it takes time - 5-10 minutes, for example, tap on "Loading Collection", to tell it to stop.. Then go a "My Downloads" where you listen to a song, even a long talk, with no connection. You can go to some feature which does require a cellular connect.

Download and Your "Shortcuts"

If you have bandwidth, tap the "Download" icon in the upper right corner. You will come to "Cancel | Download". Tap on "Download". Wait until it goes away. Now your tap the "Shortcuts" icon in lowest icon:

Saved to "Shortcuts"

If you play from the "Shortcuts", you will listen at a point where you left off. But if you play from "My Downloads" they all start at the beginning. Tap, for example, a long to talk, you can listen to it for ten minutes. Tap the "Shortcuts" icon. A message will come up: "Saved to Shortcuts". At a later time, you tap on "Shortcuts", tap the long talk, and it plays taking you to the 10 minutes marker where you listened before. Continue you listening, say, 10 minutes. Tap the "Shortcuts". You will get two "Shortcuts" entries, the most recent at top. One at the 10 minutes markers, and one with 20 minute marker. "Shortcuts"s entries are made at the top. You could click the 1O minutes marker, farther down, as a place where you want to take time to "study".

You can go to "Shortcuts". Tap on the "Delete" icon (upper right corner). It will take you to a "X" icon, on by each entry. Tap the lowest version of "Shortcuts" entry, the "X" icon. It will go away!
